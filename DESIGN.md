# Design System: Samson Dental Center

This design system is derived from the core colors and typography of the Samson Dental Center logo. It prioritizes high-contrast clarity and a modern, professional aesthetic.

---

## 1. Colors: Logo-Inspired Palette

The palette is rooted in the high-contrast monochromatic foundation of the logo, punctuated by the vibrant "Samson Red."

*   **Primary Red (`#df1e26`)**: The signature vibrant red from the logo's flourish. Use this for primary CTAs, active states, and important accents.
*   **Primary Dark (`#1a1a1a`)**: The deep charcoal/black used for the "SAMSON" text. Use this for main headings and dark-themed components.
*   **Secondary Grey (`#9ca3af`)**: The neutral grey from the "DENTAL CENTER" text. Use this for subheadings, borders, and secondary text.
*   **Surface (`#ffffff`)**: Pure white background for a clean, professional clinical feel.
*   **Surface Muted (`#f3f4f6`)**: A light grey used for background transitions and subtle sectioning.

## 3. General Principles

*   **High Contrast**: Maintain a high contrast ratio between text and backgrounds to match the logo's legibility.
*   **Bold Accents**: Use the Primary Red sparingly to draw attention to critical interactions (e.g., "Book Now").
*   **Professional Restraint**: Use the Secondary Grey and Primary Dark to create a sense of trust and authority.
