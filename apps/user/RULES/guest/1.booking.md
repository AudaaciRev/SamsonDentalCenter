# Guest Rules & Workflows

Guidelines for users booking without an account.

## 1. Booking Workflow
- **Steps**: 4-Step Wizard (Service ➔ Date & Time ➔ Contact Info ➔ Review).
- **Service Restriction**: Guests can **ONLY** book General Services. Specialized services are hidden or restricted to "Invite Only/Registered."
- **Session Security**: Uses a `guest_session_id` stored in `sessionStorage` to manage temporary slot holds during the booking process.
- **Sanitization**: Phone numbers are automatically stripped of non-numeric characters before submission.

## 2. Status & Confirmation
- **Initial Status**: Always `PENDING`.
- **Identity Snapshot**: First, Last, Middle, and Suffix names are snapshotted into the `appointments` table.
- **Verification Rule**: Guest bookings are **NOT** secured until the user clicks the verification link in their email.
- **Expiry**: Verification links expire after **15 minutes**. If not verified, the slot is released.

## 3. Rescheduling & Cancellation
- **Self-Service**: Guests can only reschedule or cancel via the unique link sent to their verified email.
- **24-Hour Policy**: Subject to the standard 24-hour notice requirement for rescheduling.
- **Token Based**: All actions are secured via a JWT-based `token` in the URL; no password required.
