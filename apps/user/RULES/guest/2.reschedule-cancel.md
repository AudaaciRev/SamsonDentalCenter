# Guest Actions: Reschedule & Cancel

Rules for guests managing their verified appointments.

## 1. Action Access
- **No Account Required**: Guests manage appointments via an "Action Token" sent to their verified email.
- **Verification Requirement**: A guest cannot reschedule or cancel an appointment that has not yet been verified via the initial "Confirm Booking" link.

## 2. Rescheduling Rules
- **Slot Availability**: Guests can only reschedule to available `General` slots.
- **Identity Retention**: The original name parts used for the guest booking are strictly retained during a reschedule.

## 3. Cancellation Policy
- **Notice Enforcement**: Just like Users, Guests are subject to the 24-hour notice rule.
- **Completion**: Once a guest cancels, their action token is invalidated, and the appointment status is marked as `CANCELLED`.
